🧑🏻‍💻 My name is Weijie Li, a Ph.D. candidate in Software Engineering at Soochow University in Suzhou, China, where I have been pursuing my doctoral degree since 2025 under the supervision of [Prof. Yu Hong](https://scst.suda.edu.cn/0c/f9/c11250a527609/page.htm) and co-supervision of [Prof. Zhongqing Wang](https://wangzqsuda.github.io)

🤔 My current research revolves around common-sense reasoning, dialogue generation, and role-playing, with a particular interest in enabling machines to understand and respond to nuanced human communication in more natural and contextually aware ways.

❌ Beyond the lab, I live by a few simple boundaries: I don't smoke, I don't drink alcohol, and I have a notable pet peeve—being rushed during meals. For me, mealtime is a moment to pause and savor, not something to be hurried through.

🏖️ In my leisure time, I am an avid anime fan with a broad taste. My watchlist ranges from iconic shonen titles like Demon Slayer (鬼灭之刃), Solo Leveling (我独自升级), Bleach (死神), Naruto (火影忍者), and One Piece (海贼王), to fantasy epics such as Shana of the Blazing Eyes (灼眼的夏娜), Sword Art Online (刀剑神域), Accel World (加速世界), Fullmetal Alchemist (钢之炼金术士), Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation (无职转生). I also enjoy mystery series like Death Note (死亡笔记) and Detective Conan (名侦探柯南), slice-of-life stories such as Frieren: Beyond Journey's End (葬送的芙莉莲) and Natsume's Book of Friends (夏目友人帐), as well as tearjerkers like CLANNAD and Your Lie in April (四月是你的谎言). I'm equally drawn to mecha (GUNDAM (高达)) and sports anime, with favorites including Kuroko's Basketball (黑子的篮球), Haikyuu!! (排球少年), The Prince of Tennis (网球王子), and Blue Lock (蓝色监狱).

📺 When I'm not watching anime, I also enjoy Chinese historical romance dramas like Blossom (九重紫), Chasing Jade (逐玉), and Veil of Shadows (月鳞绮纪). As for music, I gravitate toward love songs and ancient-style (古风) music, both of which offer a quiet, reflective counterpoint to my daily research routines.
